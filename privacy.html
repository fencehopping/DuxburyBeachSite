Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Shed Labs Studio handles information for the Duxbury Beach app and website. If you have questions, contact nick@shedlabs.studio.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to provide, such as an email address when you contact us, request updates, configure alerts, or use features that require account or notification support.

The app may process device information needed to operate core features, including approximate location for map and beach-related functionality, notification preferences, and app usage diagnostics.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide beach status, maps, alerts, guide content, support responses, product updates, app reliability improvements, and safety or abuse prevention.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate hosting, analytics, notifications, email, app infrastructure, and customer support, or when required by law.

Location and Map Features

Location-enabled features are used to make maps and beach context more useful. You can manage location permissions in your device settings. Some features may be limited if location access is disabled.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing emails using unsubscribe links where provided. You can manage push notifications, location access, and other app permissions through your device settings.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
